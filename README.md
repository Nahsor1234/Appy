
# PulsyVibe | AI Powered Mood Curations

Sync your mood with the perfect playlist instantly.

**Created by NashFire** (Nash & Fire)

## How it works
1. **Describe your vibe**: Type in your current mood, activity, or atmosphere.
2. **Set the count**: Choose how many tracks you want in your curated list.
3. **Sync**: PulsyVibe leverages a high-availability AI engine to curate a unique list of real, searchable songs.
4. **Listen**: Open the results directly on Spotify or YouTube.

## AI Intelligence
This app uses a resilient hierarchy of cutting-edge AI models via OpenRouter to ensure your vibes are always synchronized:
- **Primary Engine**: Arcee-AI Trinity Large (Preview)
- **Fallbacks**: 
  - Google Gemini 2.0 Flash Lite
  - Meta Llama 3.3 (70B)
  - Qwen 3 Next (80B)
  - Google Gemma 3 (27B)
  - DeepSeek Chat
  - Mistral 7B
  - Microsoft Phi-3 Mini

## Features
- **Organic Sonic Curation**: Multi-model AI logic that understands complex emotional signals.
- **Vibe History**: Keep track of your previous synchronizations locally on your device.
- **Themed Experience**: Three distinct visual modes: Light Teal, Emerald, and Ocean High.
- **Fluid UI**: GPU-accelerated animations and glassmorphism design.
- **Native Android Support**: Built with Capacitor for seamless APK installation on mobile devices (ARM v8 64-bit).
